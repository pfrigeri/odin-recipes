Shrimp and Sugar Snap Peas

This shrimp and sugar snap peas recipe makes a quick and easy pasta dish with a little bite that will make anyone look like a gourmet chef. Adjust the chili oil to your preferred spice level.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package linguini pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili oil
  • 1 ½ pounds medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound sugar snap peas
  • 2 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ cups dry white wine
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup pasta water.

  2. Heat olive oil and chili oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add shrimp, sugar snap peas, and garlic. Cook and stir 2 minutes, until shrimp are mostly opaque. Transfer mixture to a plate; set aside.

  3. Add wine to the wok; bring to a boil. Cook until reduced by 1/3. Return shrimp mixture to the wok, and stir in reserved pasta water. Continue to cook and stir until shrimp are opaque.

  4. Remove wok from heat; mix in basil, butter, and lemon juice. Toss with linguine, and serve.
